About Me

I am a passionate Computer Science student and Frontend Developer with hands-on experience in building responsive, user-friendly web interfaces. I specialize in transforming ideas into Functional and visually appealing web applications using modern frontend technologies

During my SIWES industrial training at AXIA HUB, I gained practical industry experience working on real-world projects, including a School Management System, where I developed the Student Registration and login module. I am motivated, detail-oriented, and eager to grow as a software developer by contributing to meaningful projects, learning from experienced professionals, and building solutions that create real impact.
